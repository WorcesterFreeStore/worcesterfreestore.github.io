Donation Bins

Come drop off items at our donation bin on 13 Cambridge Street. This bin accepts bags of shoes, clothing, and purses.

Donating At The Store

Donate at the free store Tue 3-7, Fri 10-2, Sat 12-4. Below is the list of items we currently accept. As we grow we hope to add larger items that we carry.

Bulk Donations

Please call us at 508-556-1115 or email us at info@worcesterfreestore.org for bulk donations.
