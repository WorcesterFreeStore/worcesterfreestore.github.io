tool and instrument free rental

Our tool and musical instrument rentals will enable all to build the things they need and create the music they love. These rentals will be free with suggested donations.

Bigger Space

The Worcester Free Store is looking to grow. We are currently searching for a 1000-2000 square foot space. This means more clothes, household items, furniture and large appliances.

Recycling and Scrapping

Our Recycling program will offer the residents of worcester a place to recycle items like appliances, tires, and computers. Items that are useable would be housed in the free store. Unusable items would be recycled.
